Hometown Web Page

Tampa, FL

Location

Tampa is located in Florida in the central area of the state. It is located right on the coast of Florida and bordered by the Gulf Of Mexico.

About

Weather

Tampa's temperature has never exceeded 99 degrees.

Population

As of October 2020, the population of Tampa is about 400,000 people. It is the 3rd largest city in Florida (after Miami and Jacksonville) and the 48th most populous city in the United States.

Sports

Tampa is home to a couple sports teams. The baseball team is the Tampa Bay Rays, the hockey team is The Lightning, and the football is The Buccaneers. I believe all of these teams have won a championship.
